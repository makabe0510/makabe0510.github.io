## About

I am a robotics researcher working on reconfigurable robot systems, modular robot hardware, and robot mechanisms that integrate mechanical design, sensing, and behavior generation.

## Research Interests

- Reconfigurable and transformable robot systems
- Modular robot hardware and actuator modules
- Humanoid robot mechanisms
- Robot design automation and model management
- Integration of mechanical design, perception, and behavior control


## Selected Projects

### Component-Type Reconfigurable Robot Systems

![Component-type reconfigurable robot system](/assets/images/component_type_robot.png)

I develop component-type reconfigurable robot systems that can change their body structures according to tasks and environments.  
This project focuses on the integration of detachable mechanisms, robot body components, communication interfaces, and robot models for scalable reconfiguration of robot systems.
I also develop screw-coupling detachable robot modules that mechanically and electrically connect robot body components.  
The goal is to realize robust and scalable detachable mechanisms for reconfigurable robots while keeping the hardware simple and manufacturable.

### Amphibious and Environment-Adaptive Humanoid Platforms

![Amphibious humanoid platform](/assets/images/amphibious_humanoid.png)

I develop humanoid robot platforms that can operate across different environments, including land and underwater environments.  
This project explores robot body design, waterproofing systems, sensor integration, and whole-body behavior acquisition for robots that adapt their bodies and functions to environmental constraints.

### Humanoid-Vehicle Transformer Platforms

![Humanoid-vehicle transformer platform](/assets/images/humanoid_vehicle_transformer.png)

I develop transformable humanoid platforms that transition between humanoid and vehicle-like body configurations.  
This project investigates how robot body structures, distributed sensors, and behavior generation methods can be integrated for robots with variable body configurations.

### Modular Servo and Joint Mechanisms

![Modular servo and joint mechanism](/assets/images/modular_servo_joint.png)

I develop compact servo and joint mechanisms for modular and task-adaptive robots.  
This project includes two-speed gear transmission, joint lock mechanisms, worm-gear servo modules, and 3D-printable actuator modules for robot systems that require adaptable body structures.
